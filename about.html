Meet the Team

Sherry Ashcraft (Mom)

Sherry is the fearless heart of Spirit Seekers. A lifelong believer in the paranormal, she brings emotional insight, protective instincts, and intuition to every investigation. She's the anchor — calmly navigating haunted encounters and keeping the team grounded.

Amber Brannan (Daughter)

Amber is the bold adventurer. With a passion for exploring abandoned places and an eye for tech, she's the one who captures every shadow, whisper, and unexplained occurrence. Together with her mom, she’s chasing the truth behind the unknown.

Our Mission

Spirit Seekers is more than a show — it’s a journey. As a mother-daughter duo, Sherry and Amber explore haunted locations across Texas, sharing stories of fear, history, and connection with the supernatural. No gimmicks. No scripts. Just two people brave enough to face what others avoid.
